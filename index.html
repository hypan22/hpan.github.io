Welcome to the 2023 INFORMS Annual Meeting!

In addition to thousands of presentations - united by a common theme of “Harnessing the data revolution ... all with the power of OR/MS!” - and numerous networking opportunities at the meeting, attendees are encouraged to take advantage of Phoenix’s many interesting sights and experiences.
